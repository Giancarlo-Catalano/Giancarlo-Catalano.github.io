🛠️ RSS Link Builder

👋 Welcome! Use this page to set up how your news feeds will look. Every time you make a change, a new link will be created automatically. Make sure this page and `viewer.html` are saved in the exact same folder!

1. Add News Feeds

Paste the web addresses (URIs) of the RSS feeds you want to show.

2. Appearance Settings

How many columns side-by-side? (1 to 5) Long articles will be cut short to fit nicely on the screen.

3. Your Embeddable Link

Copy this text and use it as your link. It contains all your settings bundled into a secret code.

Note about memory: Saving stores these settings to your specific internet browser on this specific laptop. If you use a different computer, or clear your internet history, your saved setup will disappear!
